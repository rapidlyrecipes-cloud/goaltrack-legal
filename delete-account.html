Delete Your Account & Data

GoalTrack — Live World Cup Scores, Standings & Bracket

You can permanently delete your GoalTrack account and the personal data associated with it at any time. Most of GoalTrack works without an account; this page applies to users who created one via email or Google sign-in.

How to request deletion

  1. Email support@getgoaltracker.com from the email address linked to your GoalTrack account.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete my GoalTrack account".
  3. We verify ownership and process the deletion within 30 days, then send you a confirmation.

What data is deleted

DataStatus on deletion
Email addressDeleted
Display nameDeleted
Favorite teams & in-app preferencesDeleted
Notification settingsDeleted
Authentication recordsDeleted

What may be retained

  • Purchase & subscription records held by our payment processors (Google Play / RevenueCat) may be retained where required for legal, tax, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes.
  • Encrypted backups may persist briefly and are automatically purged within 90 days.

Questions about your data? Contact support@getgoaltracker.com.
